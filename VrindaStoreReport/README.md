Vrinda Store Report 2022

Overview:
This report provides a summary of Vrinda Store's performance in 2022. It highlights sales, orders, demographics, and channel insights.

Key Insights:
- Women contributed 64% of total sales, men 36%.
- Orders were mostly delivered (92%), with 3% cancelled, 3% returned, and 2% refunded.
- Maharashtra and Karnataka were top-performing states.
- Amazon generated the highest orders (35%), followed by Flipkart (22%).
- Adults were the main buyers, especially women (34.59%).
- Orders and sales peaked around March, showing steady trends throughout the year.

Purpose:
To analyze sales performance, customer demographics, and order distribution across channels and regions.
